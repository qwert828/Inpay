About InPay

Building Zambia's Financial Infrastructure

InPay is a Zambian-founded and operated fintech company on a mission to make digital payments accessible to every merchant and every customer in Zambia — regardless of which mobile money network they use.

Our Mission

To unify Zambia's fragmented mobile money ecosystem into a single, seamless payment rail — so that any merchant can accept any payment, and any customer can pay anywhere.

The Problem We Are Solving

Zambia has five mobile money networks serving over 10 million registered accounts. Yet most merchants today can only accept payments from one or two networks. The result is lost sales, frustrated customers, and a payment system that works against financial inclusion.

A customer with Airtel Money cannot pay a merchant who only accepts MTN. A merchant who wants to accept all networks must register with five different companies, maintain five accounts, and reconcile five separate statements. This is the problem InPay solves.
