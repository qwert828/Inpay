Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a setup fee?
No. InPay has zero setup fees. You sign up, get your API key and QR code, and start accepting payments immediately.
When do I get paid?
Settlements are processed daily. Funds collected from customers are transferred to your registered mobile money account every business day.
Can I accept payments from all networks on the free plan?
Yes. Even on the free Starter plan, your customers can pay using any of the 5 Zambian mobile money networks — MTN, Airtel, Zamtel, SpeedPay and ZedMobile.
What is a webhook?
A webhook is an automatic notification sent to your system when a payment is completed. It allows your website or app to automatically update when a customer pays you.
Is InPay licensed by the Bank of Zambia?
InPay is currently in the process of applying for a Payment Service Provider license from the Bank of Zambia. Sandbox operations are available while the license application is in progress.
